About Us

We are Sri Lankan Safest Driving – your trusted travel partner for safe, comfortable and memorable journeys across Sri Lanka, from the golden beaches of Hikkaduwa to the misty hills and ancient cities.

Who We Are

For years, Sri Lankan Safest Driving has helped travellers explore Sri Lanka with confidence. We specialize in private tours, airport transfers, long day trips and tailor‑made holiday plans, always with safety and genuine Sri Lankan hospitality at the center.

Based in Hikkaduwa and Colombo, our experienced local team knows the best routes, hidden gems and trusted partners around the island, so you can relax and enjoy the journey.

  • Friendly, professional and well‑experienced drivers
  • Fully insured and well‑maintained vehicles
  • Customized itineraries for couples, families and groups
  • Support from the first enquiry until you return home

Our Promise to You

Every trip with Sri Lankan Safest Driving is designed to be safe, comfortable and honest. We take time to understand your plans, give clear pricing with no hidden charges, and provide guidance like a local friend – not just a driver.

Whether you need a one‑day tour, a full island round trip or long‑stay villa and vehicle arrangements, we are ready to help you experience Sri Lanka in the best and safest way.
