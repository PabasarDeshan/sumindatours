Your Home Away from Home in Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha

Welcome to Your Home Away from Home in Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha, Galle

Nestled in the peaceful enclave of Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha (Brown's Road), just 1 kilometer away from Karapitiya Teaching Hospital and a short 3-kilometer drive from Galle Central Bus Stand, this charming house offers the perfect blend of convenience and comfort. Surrounded by a friendly residential neighborhood, it's an ideal choice for those seeking a cozy and well-appointed retreat in Galle.

Bedroom Details

  • Three inviting bedrooms await you, ensuring a restful night's sleep for up to six guests.
  • Two spacious King Size beds and two comfortable Double Beds offer ample sleeping options.
  • Two of the bedrooms are equipped with air conditioning, perfect for keeping cool during warm Sri Lankan evenings.
  • One bedroom features an attached bathroom for added privacy and convenience.

Bathrooms

Two well-maintained bathrooms are at your disposal, both equipped with hot water for a relaxing and refreshing shower experience.

Fully Equipped Kitchen & Household Items

  • The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances, including a refrigerator and a washing machine.
  • Two large wardrobes provide ample storage space for your belongings.
  • A comfortable sofa with a coffee table in the living area is perfect for relaxing with family or friends.

Garage and Rooftop

  • A garage is available for your convenience, providing secure parking for your vehicle.
  • The rooftop is an inviting space where you can soak in the scenic views of the surrounding area, enjoy a cup of tea, or simply unwind under the clear skies.

WiFi

Stay connected with high-speed WiFi available (ADSL-SLT), ensuring you can keep in touch with loved ones or stay productive during your stay.

This Galle residence offers more than just a place to stay; it offers a glimpse into the welcoming lifestyle of the local community. Whether you're exploring the historic attractions of Galle Fort or simply enjoying the tranquility of the neighborhood, this house is your perfect home base. Book your stay now and experience the charm of Anagarika Dharmapala Mawatha in Galle.
