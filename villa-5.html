Your Home Away from Home in Galle

Welcome to Your Home Away from Home in Galle

Nestled in the picturesque city of Galle, our charming villa offers the perfect blend of comfort and convenience. Located in a tranquil neighborhood, it's an ideal choice for those seeking a peaceful and well-appointed retreat in the heart of Galle.

Bedroom & Bathroom Details

Our villa boasts three cozy bedrooms, each equipped with air conditioning and an attached bathroom featuring hot water facilities for your ultimate comfort. Whether you're visiting as a family or group of friends, our villa provides ample sleeping options for all.

Fan

For those who prefer a natural breeze, fans are provided in every room to keep you cool and comfortable.

Fiber Optic Internet

Stay connected and productive with high-speed fiber optic internet. Whether you need to work or simply stay in touch with loved ones, you'll have a reliable connection.

Balcony

Enjoy the beauty of Galle from your own private balcony. It's the perfect spot to savor your morning coffee or unwind in the evenings while taking in the scenic views.

Garden

Our villa is surrounded by a lush garden, creating a peaceful and natural environment for you to enjoy. You can take a leisurely stroll or simply relax amidst the greenery.

Outdoor Bathroom

In addition to the en-suite bathrooms, we offer an outdoor bathroom with hot water facilities, providing a unique and refreshing experience for our guests.

CCTV

Your security is our priority. We have CCTV cameras installed to ensure your safety and peace of mind during your stay.

Fully Furnished

Our villa comes fully furnished, so you can feel at home from the moment you arrive. We've taken care of all the details to make your stay as comfortable as possible.

Price

The villa is available at a competitive rate of 300,000 LKR per month, excluding bills. It's a great value for the comfort and amenities provided.

Our villa is conveniently situated in Galle, allowing you to explore the historic attractions of the city, beautiful beaches, and the vibrant local culture. We invite you to book your stay and experience the tranquility and comfort it offers — your perfect home away from home in this beautiful city.
