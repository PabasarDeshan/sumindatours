VILLA - 3 : Hikkaduwa

3-Bedroom Coastal Paradise

Discover Coastal Paradise at its Finest

Nestled along the pristine shores of Hikkaduwa, this exquisite 3-bedroom villa offers a quintessential beachfront escape. With its perfect blend of modern amenities and tropical charm, it promises an idyllic retreat for those seeking sun, sea, and serenity.

Bedrooms

This villa features three inviting bedrooms, each thoughtfully designed to provide the utmost comfort and relaxation. Whether you're traveling with family or friends, these tranquil spaces ensure a restful night's sleep.

Bathrooms

Two well-appointed bathrooms are at your disposal, complete with hot water for a rejuvenating shower experience. They are maintained to impeccable standards to ensure your comfort throughout your stay.

Air-Conditioned Room

Escape the tropical heat in the comfort of an air-conditioned room, where you can unwind and recharge after a day of beach activities.

Kitchen

A fully-equipped kitchen awaits the culinary enthusiasts, furnished with modern appliances and ample counter space. Prepare delicious meals using fresh local ingredients or simply enjoy your morning coffee as you plan your day's adventures.

Hot Water

After a day of swimming and sunbathing, you can rinse off the salt and sand in the comfort of a hot shower, ensuring a refreshing experience.

WiFi

Stay connected to the world with high-speed WiFi available throughout the villa. Share your Hikkaduwa memories with loved ones or catch up on work if needed.

Washing Machine

For your convenience, a washing machine is provided, allowing you to freshen up your beachwear and clothing during your stay.

This Hikkaduwa villa is not just a place to stay; it's a gateway to beachfront bliss. Step outside onto the golden sands, take a dip in the azure waters, or simply relax on your private terrace, savoring the gentle sea breeze. The vibrant culture and stunning natural beauty of Hikkaduwa await your exploration. Book your stay now and let the coastal paradise envelop you in relaxation and adventure.
