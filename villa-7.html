Welcome to Your Charming Villa in Gonapinuwala

Nestled in the serene village of Gonapinuwala, just 4 kilometers from the bustling Hikkaduwa to Baddegama main road, this cozy villa is the perfect escape for those seeking a tranquil and well-appointed retreat on the beautiful island of Sri Lanka.

Bedroom Details

This villa offers two inviting bedrooms, accommodating up to four guests comfortably. Each bedroom is thoughtfully furnished, providing a restful night's sleep.

Bathroom Details

The villa boasts a well-maintained bathroom with hot water, ensuring a relaxing and refreshing shower experience.

Fully Equipped Kitchen

The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances, making it easy for you to prepare your favorite meals during your stay.

WiFi

Stay connected with high-speed WiFi, ensuring you can keep in touch with loved ones or stay productive during your visit.

Washing Machine

For your convenience, a washing machine is available, allowing you to refresh your clothes after a day of adventure.
