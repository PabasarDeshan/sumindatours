VILLA - 1 : Unawatuna

Stunning Family Suite in Unawatuna's Heart

Discover the allure of Unawatuna from our splendid Family Suite (Apartment) nestled right in the heart of this picturesque coastal town. This fully furnished 2-bedroom suite is perfect for up to 4 guests, making it an ideal choice for tourists and expatriates seeking a tranquil and convenient retreat.

Spacious and Stylish

The suite boasts two elegantly designed bedrooms to ensure a restful night's sleep. A shared bathroom, a well-equipped kitchen, and a cozy living area complete the interior, offering a comfortable and stylish stay.

Tranquil Paradise

Set in a serene and peaceful environment, our Family Suite provides the perfect escape from the daily grind. The tranquil neighborhood adds to the sense of calm and relaxation, making it an ideal getaway for those seeking tranquility.

Modern Comforts

We understand the importance of modern amenities during your stay. Our suite comes with essential facilities and services to enhance your comfort:

  • Complimentary Wifi: Stay connected with high-speed internet access.
  • Convenient Washing Machine: Keep your wardrobe fresh and clean.
  • Handy Refrigerator: Store your favorite snacks and drinks with ease.
  • Fully Equipped Kitchen: Prepare delicious meals with all the necessary equipment.
  • Hot Water: Enjoy refreshing showers with continuous hot water in the bathroom.

Convenient Transport Options

We offer hassle-free transportation options to enhance your stay:

  • Airport Shuttle: Arrange for smooth transfers to and from the airport.
  • Proximity to Unawatuna Beach: Our suite is conveniently located near the world-famous Unawatuna Beach, allowing you easy access to sun, sea, and sand.

Whether you're a tourist eager to explore Unawatuna's beauty or an expatriate staff member seeking a comfortable and convenient stay, our Family Suite is your home away from home. Experience the tranquility of our neighborhood, relish modern amenities, and bask in the proximity to the beach. Reserve your stay with us and make your Unawatuna adventure truly unforgettable.
